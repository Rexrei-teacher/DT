Step-by-Step Optimization (SI Units)

Note: Ultimate Strength of spaghetti (\(\sigma_{u}\)) \(\approx 15,000,000 \text{ Pa}\).

1. Identify Member Force (\(F\)):
Find the Force in Newtons (\(N\)) from JHU.
2. Calculate Area (\(A\)) in \(m^2\):
$$A = \frac{F}{\sigma_{u}} = \frac{F}{15,000,000}$$
3. Find Spaghetti Count (\(n\)):
If area of one strand is (\(a\)): $$n = \frac{A}{a}$$
